Etch-A-Sketch

Designed by Josiah Holmes


Welcome to the digital Etch-A-Sketch! To use it, simply hover your mouse inside the gray screen and watch the magic happen! To start a new screen, click the "Reset" button below, enter how large you want the screen to be, and let the Etch-A-Sketch do the rest! Click the "Terminate" button to make the Etch-A-Sketch disappear, and "Come Back!" to make it come back! Have fun and draw something cool!
